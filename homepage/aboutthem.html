Fritto

Orange Tabby, born in Hawaii...Fritto remains as ditzy and chill as he was when he was just a kitten.

I first adopted him back in 2018 while I was stationed at Hickam Air Force Base, and the little guy's been traveling along with me ever since. While he hates car rides with a passion and can hardly stand flights, Fritto has probably traveled more in the past few years than most people do in their lifetimes. Never afraid of strangers (for better or worse), the second he meets someone new he'll be meowing along and wondering why you haven't given him any pets yet.

While he can be pretty vocal when he wants to, he's usually just sunbathing or napping on the couch.

Lola

A relatively newer addition to the family, Lola is a Calico who, of the two, can be seen as the "scardy" cat.

First adpoted back in 2022 while I was stationed in Arizona, Lola grew up with a viral strain that has permentantly affected her eyes - while the virus still affects her, it doesn't do much to stop her from bring the snugglebug she is. Weary of strangers, she'll be the first to hide whenever someone new is in the area. Once she warms up to you though, she'll constantly keep bugging you for attention and head scratches.

While normally quiet, her curiosity and energy make it hard to not notice her when she wants to play.
