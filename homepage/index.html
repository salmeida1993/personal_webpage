About Me

Hello there, my name is Stewart Almeida and welcome to my homepage! As a continuing graduate student at Northeastern's MSCS Align program, this page serves as a growing culmination of the skills and knowledge gained throughout my coursework. While modest in implementation, I hope to continue to modify this site with new quirks and added improvements for a pleasant viewing experience - both from a technical and graphical standpoint.

Prior to my enrollment in the MSCS program, I served as a Weather Officer for the U.S. Air Force for over 7 years. During that time, I personally crafted and issued weather forecasts and WWAs for various Air Force and sister service installations, studied at the Air Force Institute of Technology for my M.S. in Atmospheric Science, and even had the opportunity to deploy overseas supporting crisis response and HA/DR operations throughout Africa. From leadership, teamwork, personnel management, and a drive to complete any task at hand, the skills and experiences I gained are things I am excited to bring to any team.
